2. Third-Party Services

FramedPicture uses Apple WeatherKit for weather data. WeatherKit is Apple's own service — not a third-party API. Your location is sent to Apple's servers only to retrieve weather conditions; it is not stored, sold, or shared. Apple's privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.

No third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or social media frameworks are used.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All photos, music, and settings data remain on your device at all times.
  • The developer cannot access and does not collect any user data.
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

4. Children's Privacy

FramedPicture is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

5. Changes to This Policy

Any changes will be reflected on this page. Significant updates will also appear in the app's release notes.

2. 제3자 서비스

FramedPicture는 날씨 데이터를 위해 Apple WeatherKit을 사용합니다. WeatherKit은 Apple 자체 서비스이며 제3자 API가 아닙니다. 위치 정보는 날씨 조회 목적으로만 Apple 서버에 전송되며 저장·판매·공유되지 않습니다. Apple 개인정보처리방침: apple.com/legal/privacy.

광고 네트워크, 분석 SDK, 충돌 리포터, 소셜 미디어 프레임워크 등 제3자 서비스를 일절 사용하지 않습니다.
